Maison

A Parisian maison

Loubelle was founded in Paris as a house of haute couture, where each gown is composed by hand in our atelier. We believe in the tension between absolute white and profound black.

Every piece is numbered, signed, and made to last beyond a single season. This is couture as a quiet statement.
